What is DPCA?

DPCA, or Dow Patient Care Association, was founded in 2012 and is a resident non-profit, non-political welfare organization that aims to bridge the gap between needy patients and healthcare.

We help pay for a variety of diagnostic tests, procedures, medications and even surgeries with amounts given that are judged on a case by case basis. Patients are screened and surveyed to ensure fair and just distributions of funds.
