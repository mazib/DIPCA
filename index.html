We care about helping patients in need.

DIPCA, or Dow International Patient Care Association, was founded in 2012 and is a resident non-profit, non-political welfare organization that aims to bridge the gap between needy patients and healthcare.

Learn More Join DIPCA

We help patients cover their expenses.

We help pay for a variety of diagnostic tests, procedures, medications and even surgeries with amounts given judged on a case by case basis. Patients are screened & surveyed to ensure fair and just distributions of funds.

Totally transparent.

All donations and contributions given to DIPCA are maintained, logged, compiled and handled properly by the treasurer. Every little bit is put to use in a responsible way.

Funded by you.

Apart from generous donations from individuals, DIPCA also organizes a variety of events and fundraisers to raise funds to help those in need.

Run by students.

Since 2012, leadership roles and responsibilities have been maintained and passed on by seniors to newer students to ensure a smooth operation.

“Today the cost of medical services is depriving the poor of many of the benefits of modern medicine. To provide everyone medical coverage without fail requires sharing in the community to bear the costs of treatments. It is highly encouraging that the students have launched a program to help the needy, poor, and helpless people. I congratulate them and pray for their success.”

Professor Muhammad Masroor / Principal, DIMC
